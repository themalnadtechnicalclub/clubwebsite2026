FOUNDATIONS

Foundations is a district-level event organized by The Malnad Technical Club since the year of its inception in 2009. Since then, it has seen successful addition of new events and has been influential in bringing talent and creativity together on a big stage. In 2019, Foundations was conducted in the college to celebrate the 10 year anniversary of the club. While events like quiz, think and ink tested the technical brilliance of the students, events like poetry, bridge construction presented a creative arena to push the limits of artistry. With events like these, The Malnad Technical Club has set the benchmark for inspiring young minds to bring out their best and drive the future forward.
